**Offered by:**  
Dr. Ehsan Yaghoubi  

**Supervisors:**

Dr. Ehsan Yaghoubi  

**Description:**  
This topic investigates **non-invasive cattle re-identification** across multiple camera views in barn environments. The goal is to match individual animals across:

- Frontal views  
- Lateral views  
- Elevated views  

The project explores **deep feature extraction**, **metric learning**, and compares **CNN- and Transformer-based backbones**.  
This work supports automated, markerless animal monitoring in precision livestock farming.
