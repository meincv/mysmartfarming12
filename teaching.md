## Master's Programme: Digital Farming (M.Sc.)

We actively contribute to the **Master's programme in Digital Farming**, which focuses on AI, robotics, and digital technologies to solve global challenges in agriculture.

### Programme Overview

**Focus Areas:**
- Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
- Agricultural Robotics & Automation
- Precision Agriculture & Livestock Farming
- Data Analytics for Agriculture
- Sustainable Digital Solutions

- **Language of instruction:** English
- **Duration:** 4 semesters (120 ECTS)
- **Location:** Campus Weihenstephan (near Munich, Bavaria)

## Student Projects & Theses

We regularly supervise Bachelor's and Master's theses on topics related to our research areas:

- Computer vision for agricultural applications
- Machine learning for crop and animal monitoring
- Time series analysis for smart grids and energy systems
- AI-based decision support systems
- Robotics and automation in agriculture

**Interested in a thesis project?** Contact us at [florian.haselbeck@hswt.de](mailto:florian.haselbeck@hswt.de) to discuss available topics aligned with your interests and our ongoing research.

## Teaching Philosophy

Our teaching approach combines:

- **Hands-on learning** with real-world datasets and agricultural systems
- **Interdisciplinary perspectives** bridging computer science and agriculture
- **Project-based education** solving actual farming challenges
- **Industry connections** preparing students for careers in agri-tech
- **Research integration** exposing students to cutting-edge developments

We believe in empowering students with both theoretical foundations and practical skills to become leaders in digital transformation of agriculture.
